# Customer Agreement (University)

Template for legal review. This document is a structured starting point and must be reviewed and approved by a qualified legal adviser before it is offered to or executed with any institution.

This Customer Agreement (the "Agreement") sets out the terms on which Mind Measure Ltd provides its wellbeing platform to a university, college or other institution (the "Institution"). It is the commercial contract between the two organisations. It is distinct from the end-user Privacy Policy and Terms of Service, which govern how individual students use the app, and from the Data Processing Agreement, which governs the processing of personal data.

## 1. Parties

This Agreement is made between:

- **Mind Measure Ltd**, a company registered in England and Wales (company number 16555431), with its registered office at 71-75 Shelton Street, Covent Garden, London, WC2H 9JQ ("Mind Measure", "we", "us", "our"); and
- the Institution identified in the Order Form (the "Institution", "you", "your").

Each is a "party" and together the "parties". This Agreement takes effect on the date of the last signature on the Order Form (the "Effective Date").

## 2. Definitions

- **Order Form**: the ordering document signed by both parties that identifies the Institution, the subscribed Services, the Fees, the term and any agreed variations to these terms.
- **Services**: the Mind Measure wellbeing platform, including the student mobile application, the institutional dashboard and any related services described in Schedule 1, as subscribed in the Order Form.
- **Authorised Users**: students and staff of the Institution permitted to access the Services in accordance with this Agreement.
- **Aggregated Data**: anonymised, aggregated insight derived from use of the Services that cannot reasonably be used to identify any individual.
- **Personal Data**, **Controller**, **Processor** and **Processing**: as defined in the UK GDPR.
- **DPA**: the Data Processing Agreement entered into between the parties, which forms part of this Agreement.
- **Fees**: the charges for the Services set out in the Order Form.

## 3. The Services and licence

Mind Measure grants the Institution a non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable right, during the term, to access and use the Services for the internal wellbeing and student support purposes described in this Agreement and the Order Form, for the number of Authorised Users subscribed.

The Institution shall not, and shall not permit any third party to: resell or commercially exploit the Services; copy, modify or create derivative works of the Services; reverse-engineer or attempt to derive the source code, models or algorithms; or use the Services to build a competing product.

Mind Measure may update and improve the Services from time to time, provided that no update will materially reduce the core functionality subscribed during the then-current term.

## 4. Institution responsibilities

The Institution shall:

- use the Services in accordance with this Agreement, the DPA and applicable law;
- ensure that its Authorised Users are eligible and that appropriate consent and safeguarding arrangements are in place, in particular for any user under the age of 18;
- nominate appropriate welfare and safeguarding contacts and keep them current;
- not use the Services, or any insight derived from them, for grading, discipline, progression decisions or any purpose that would disadvantage an individual student; and
- be responsible for the acts and omissions of its Authorised Users as if they were its own.

## 5. Fees and payment

The Institution shall pay the Fees set out in the Order Form. Unless the Order Form states otherwise:

- Fees are stated in pounds sterling (GBP) and are exclusive of VAT, which is payable in addition where applicable;
- invoices are payable within 30 days of the invoice date;
- Fees are payable annually in advance; and
- Mind Measure may charge interest on overdue amounts at 4% per annum above the Bank of England base rate.

Mind Measure may revise the Fees for any renewal term on at least 60 days' written notice before the end of the then-current term.

## 6. Term, renewal and termination

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for the initial term stated in the Order Form (typically 12 months). It renews automatically for successive periods equal to the initial term unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days before the end of the then-current term.

Either party may terminate this Agreement on written notice if the other party: commits a material breach that is not remedied within 30 days of written notice; or becomes insolvent, enters administration or ceases to carry on business.

The Institution may suspend or stop deploying the Services to new users at any time, but this does not relieve it of the obligation to pay Fees for the committed term.

## 7. Service levels and support

Mind Measure will use commercially reasonable efforts to make the Services available with high availability, excluding planned maintenance (for which reasonable notice will be given) and events outside its reasonable control. Support is provided during UK business hours by email, with target response times set out in Schedule 3.

## 8. Data protection

Each party shall comply with applicable data protection law. In respect of Personal Data of Authorised Users processed through the Services, the parties' respective roles and obligations are set out in the DPA, which is incorporated into this Agreement. In the event of conflict between this Agreement and the DPA on data protection matters, the DPA prevails.

Individual student data is never disclosed to the Institution. The Institution receives only Aggregated Data, subject to the minimum group thresholds and safeguards described in the Privacy Policy and the Services documentation.

## 9. Aggregated and anonymised data

Mind Measure may generate and use Aggregated Data to operate, develop, benchmark and improve the Services, and for research and reporting, provided that such data is anonymised and cannot reasonably be used to identify any individual or, without consent, the Institution.

## 10. Confidentiality

Each party shall keep confidential the other party's confidential information and use it only to perform this Agreement. This obligation does not apply to information that is or becomes public through no fault of the receiving party, was lawfully known before disclosure, or is required to be disclosed by law or a regulator. This clause survives termination.

## 11. Intellectual property

Mind Measure and its licensors own all intellectual property rights in the Services, including all software, models, algorithms, content and branding. Nothing in this Agreement transfers any such rights to the Institution beyond the licence expressly granted. The Institution owns its own data and branding and grants Mind Measure the limited rights needed to provide the Services.

## 12. Warranties

Mind Measure warrants that it will provide the Services with reasonable skill and care and in accordance with the documentation. The Institution warrants that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement and to deploy the Services to its Authorised Users.

Except as expressly stated, the Services are provided "as is" and Mind Measure does not warrant that they will be uninterrupted or error-free. The Services are a wellbeing support tool and do not provide medical diagnosis, clinical assessment or crisis intervention.

## 13. Limitation of liability

Nothing in this Agreement limits either party's liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, for fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation, or for any liability that cannot be limited by law.

Subject to the above, neither party is liable for indirect, special or consequential loss, or for loss of profit, revenue, goodwill or anticipated savings. Subject to the above, each party's total aggregate liability arising under or in connection with this Agreement in any 12-month period shall not exceed the total Fees paid or payable by the Institution in that period.

## 14. Indemnities

Mind Measure shall indemnify the Institution against third-party claims that the Services, as provided and used in accordance with this Agreement, infringe that third party's intellectual property rights. The Institution shall indemnify Mind Measure against claims arising from the Institution's use of the Services in breach of this Agreement or applicable law. Each indemnity is subject to prompt notice, reasonable cooperation and the indemnifying party having conduct of the defence.

## 15. Suspension

Mind Measure may suspend access to the Services where necessary to protect the security or integrity of the platform or its users, or where the Institution is materially in breach or its account is significantly overdue. Mind Measure will give notice where reasonably practicable and will restore access promptly once the cause is resolved.

## 16. Effect of termination

On termination or expiry: the licence ends and the Institution shall stop using the Services; any accrued Fees become payable; and each party shall, on request, return or delete the other's confidential information. Personal Data is returned or deleted in accordance with the DPA and the retention periods in the Privacy Policy. Aggregated Data that cannot identify any individual may be retained.

## 17. Publicity

Neither party shall use the other's name or logo in publicity without prior written consent, except that, where the Order Form permits, Mind Measure may identify the Institution as a customer in its customer lists and materials.

## 18. Force majeure

Neither party is liable for any delay or failure to perform caused by events beyond its reasonable control, including natural disasters, pandemics, government action, war, terrorism, power or internet failures, or failures of third-party providers. The affected party shall notify the other and use reasonable efforts to mitigate.

## 19. Notices

Notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and sent to the contacts and addresses stated in the Order Form, or to Mind Measure at the registered office above, marked for the attention of the Company Secretary. Notices by email are valid where receipt is acknowledged.

## 20. General

This Agreement, together with the Order Form, the DPA and the schedules, is the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes any prior arrangements on its subject matter. No variation is effective unless agreed in writing. A failure to enforce a term is not a waiver of it. If any term is held invalid, the rest remains in force. Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other's consent, save that Mind Measure may assign to a successor of its business. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership or agency. A person who is not a party has no rights under the Contracts (Rights of Third Parties) Act 1999.

## 21. Governing law and jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales, and the parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
