# Product Roadmap

The single source of truth for what is shipped, in build, and under consideration across the Mind Measure platform. The two product lines (University and Pro) share one engine but have distinct near-term priorities, so they are tracked separately below, with cross-platform and engineering work pulled into its own section.

For lower-level technical work in progress, see [Future Development](/future-development).

**Last Updated**: June 2026

---

## How to read this

**Status** values:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Shipped | Live in production |
| In build | Actively being built now |
| Next sprint | Specced and queued |
| Planned | Agreed, not yet specced in detail |
| Blocked | Built up to a point, gated on external sign-off (clinical, legal) |
| Concept | Idea captured, spec to be written |
| Research | R&D / feasibility only |
| Future | Long-term, not yet scheduled |
| Deferred | Intentionally parked for now |

### Wellbeing Protection programme (phase detail)

The university safeguarding programme that sits beneath the Weekly Reflection and the daily check-in. Foundations and the welfare timeline are live; the phases that take automated action on a student's behalf, or disclose to a third party, are deliberately held until clinical thresholds and the legal disclosure basis are signed off.

| Phase | Scope | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | Data foundations, safeguarding-contact model, superuser welfare audit panel | Shipped |
| Phase 6 | Per-student welfare timeline API, PDF export and timeline panel | Shipped |
| Phase 2 | Compound trigger engine (combine signals across check-ins into a single risk picture) | Blocked, clinical review of thresholds |
| Phase 3 | Automated Level 4 notification pipeline to university welfare contacts | Blocked, clinical + legal review of the disclosure basis (GDPR vital interests) |
| Phase 4 | Disclosure review queue with write actions (human-in-the-loop sign-off before any disclosure) | Blocked, legal review |
| Phase 5 | Onboarding consent screen in the student app | Blocked, legal review |
| Phase 7 | Real-time Level 5 crisis response | Planned |

Keyword scanning, the Bedrock safety flag, score thresholds and a full audit trail are already live and feed the reflection. The blocked phases are an organisational gate (clinical and legal), not an engineering one.

## How to maintain this document

- **Add ideas freely** to the relevant product table or Cross-platform, then refine.
- **Promote to a higher status** as a spec is written and work begins; link to the full spec.
- **When a feature ships**: move it to Recently shipped (with product and month), remove it from the roadmap tables, and ensure it is listed in [Future Development](/future-development).
- **Never delete** — move to Parked with a note instead.
- Keep the **Last Updated** date current and check status against git ground truth, not memory.
