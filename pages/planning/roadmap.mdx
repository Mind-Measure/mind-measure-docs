# Product Roadmap

The single source of truth for what is shipped, in build, and under consideration across the Mind Measure platform. The two product lines (University and Pro) share one engine but have distinct near-term priorities, so they are tracked separately below, with cross-platform and engineering work pulled into its own section.

For lower-level technical work in progress, see [Future Development](/future-development).

**Last Updated**: June 2026

---

## How to read this

**Status** values:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Shipped | Live in production |
| In build | Actively being built this sprint |
| Next sprint | Specced and queued |
| Planned | Agreed, not yet specced in detail |
| Concept | Idea captured, spec to be written |
| Research | R&D / feasibility only |
| Deferred | Intentionally parked for now |

## University — roadmap

| Initiative | Priority | Status | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weekly Reflection (production wiring) | High | In build | Personalised Sunday email summarising each student's week through their Jodie conversations. Phase 1 live: email, web view, PDF, share card, safeguarding infrastructure, mobile opt-out, and the superuser audit panel. Remaining: Tier 0 inactivity nudge and the welfare-modified reflection for negative-only weeks. [Spec](/operations/weekly-reflection) · [Safeguards](/operations/weekly-reflection-safeguards) · [Runbook](/operations/weekly-reflection-runbook) |
| Welfare monitoring (Tiers 2/3 escalation) | High | Foundation shipped; escalation pending sign-off | Tiered response framework beneath the reflection. Keyword scan, Bedrock safety flag, score thresholds and full audit trail are live. Tier 2/3 escalation to university welfare contacts (GDPR vital interests) awaits clinical review of thresholds and legal review of the disclosure basis. [Policy](/operations/weekly-reflection-safeguards) |
| Cohort Template Library | High | Next sprint | Turn one-off cohort comparisons into a shared, live-data template library scoped to each institution. Migrates `localStorage` views to a `saved_cohort_templates` table, adds custom titles and author attribution, publish-to-library, live data on load, and a historical overlay across time periods. |
| Student Ambassador Platform | High | Concept | Ambassador signup and onboarding, branded social-asset library, referral tracking, payout dashboard and admin management, to drive organic student acquisition through peer networks. |
| Jodie prompt improvements (university) | Medium | Planned | Additive prompts (named events, sleep/energy probe, positive anchor, social-connection probe, week framing) that enrich the data feeding the Weekly Reflection. Requires a prompt version bump and regression testing; does not change the check-in structure. |
| Streak rewards | Medium | Under review | Voucher rewards for consistent check-ins. On hold pending a mechanism that reinforces intrinsic motivation rather than undermining it. Revenue case (retailer sponsorship) remains valid; revisit once Weekly Reflection engagement data is available. |
| Peer support network | Low | Future | Evolve the buddy system into trained peer supporters with guided conversation prompts and an institutional feedback loop. |

## Pro — roadmap

| Initiative | Priority | Status | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Institutional Alerts | High | Phase 1 shipped; Phase 2 in build | Severity-tagged broadcast channel from company admins into the employee app (welfare / info / notice / urgent), with push notifications for higher tiers. Driven by the PwC Middle East pilot, where staff need a single source of truth for whether the office is open. Phase 2 adds audience targeting; welfare-tier alerts always ship with support links. Reach exposed as anonymous aggregate counts only. [Spec](/planning/institutional-alerts) |
| Weekly Reflection (Pro) | High | Shipped (Jun 2026) | Same reflection pipeline as university, brought to parity in `mind-measure-pro-core`, with a superuser toggle, opt-in gating, and an opt-in toggle in the employee app settings. |
| Mental health advocates + structured EAP | Medium | Shipped (Jun 2026) | Per-office mental health advocates and structured EAP programmes feeding Jodie's signposting knowledge, so employee-facing support routing matches each employer's provision. |

## Cross-platform & engineering

Work that spans both product lines, or sits in the shared engine and tooling.

| Initiative | Priority | Status | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Conversational Insight v2 | High | Shipped both lines | Cohort-targeted, ask-once questions woven into check-ins with an always-on dashboard. Phases 1–4 live: audience targeting, per-user ask-once rotation, fully anonymised responses, and per-session prompt resolution at check-in start on both apps. Standalone Insight Hubs live at insight.mindmeasure.co.uk and insight.mindmeasurepro.com. Phase 5 (aggregate export + thread comparison across runs) deferred. [Spec](/planning/conversational-insight-v2) |
| Per-institution agent personalisation + memory | High | Shipped both lines | Live session context injected into each institution's Jodie prompt; custom check-in questions replace an exchange rather than extending length; 4–6 exchange default pacing. |
| Downloadable Wellbeing Reports (enhanced) | Medium | V1 shipped; enhancements blocked | 14/30/90-day reports with executive summary, transcript snippets and charts are live. Clinical enhancements (therapist-reviewed language, curated suggestions, baseline comparison with clinical context) await professional input. |
| Score reveal interaction | Medium | Spec written | A short "preparing your results" transition followed by an animated gauge reveal, to give the score perceived weight without feeling like a delay (1–2 seconds). |
| Text-based check-in | Medium | Concept | A chat alternative to voice using the same Jodie persona and flow, for quiet environments and lower data usage. |
| Auth token standardisation | Medium | Planning | Align the university and Pro mobile apps on the `cognitoApiClient` pattern (proven in Pro), then extract a shared auth module. |
| Load testing | Medium | Not started | Exercise the full check-in flow at 50/100/200 concurrent users; identify and fix Bedrock/Rekognition throttling and Aurora connection limits, then set an ongoing baseline. |
| Investor dashboard | Medium | Concept | Growth, adoption, revenue/cost and platform-health view. Needs design; no Figma yet. |
| Multi-language support | Low | Future | Jodie voice, Bedrock analysis, mobile UI and knowledge bases in the user's preferred language for international populations. |
| Voice provider abstraction (PersonaPlex) | Low (R&D) | Research | Provider-agnostic voice interface so ElevenLabs can be swapped for OpenAI Realtime, Cartesia, or self-hosted NVIDIA PersonaPlex. PersonaPlex offers ~100x cost reduction at scale (MIT-licensed weights); break-even at ~10,000 minutes/month. |
| API for institutional systems | Low | Future | Anonymised, aggregated data API plus SSO and risk-escalation webhooks for enterprise integration. |

## How to maintain this document

- **Add ideas freely** to the relevant product table or Cross-platform, then refine.
- **Promote to a higher status** as a spec is written and work begins; link to the full spec.
- **When a feature ships**: move it to Recently shipped (with product and month), remove it from the roadmap tables, and ensure it is listed in [Future Development](/future-development).
- **Never delete** — move to Parked with a note instead.
- Keep the **Last Updated** date current and check status against git ground truth, not memory.
