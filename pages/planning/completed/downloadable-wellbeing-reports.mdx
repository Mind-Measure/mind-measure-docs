# Downloadable Wellbeing Reports

**Status**: V1 Complete  
**Completed**: February 2026  
**Platforms**: University mobile (`mobile.mindmeasure.app`), Pro mobile (`mobile.mindmeasurepro.app`)

---

## What We Built

Detailed downloadable wellbeing reports covering 14, 30, and 90-day periods. Users can generate and download a comprehensive summary of their check-in history.

## V1 Capabilities

- **Executive summary** — high-level overview of the user's wellbeing trajectory
- **Mood score trends** — charted over the selected period with rolling averages
- **Key themes** — recurring positive and negative themes identified across check-ins
- **Transcript snippets** — notable quotes from conversations, contextualising the data
- **Driver analysis** — what's contributing positively and negatively to wellbeing
- **Visual charts** — mood trends, theme frequency, score distribution
- **PDF generation** — downloadable report formatted for sharing or personal records

## Technical Implementation

- Report generation via `/api/generate-report` endpoint
- Analysis powered by AWS Bedrock (Claude Haiku 4.5)
- Data aggregated from `fusion_outputs` and `assessment_sessions` tables
- PDF rendering via server-side generation
- Available from the user's profile/wellness tab in both mobile apps

## What's Next (V2 — Blocked)

Further enhancements require professional input from therapists:

- Therapist-reviewed content guidance and language
- Actionable wellbeing suggestions (curated by clinicians, not AI-generated)
- Comparison to personal baseline with clinical context
- Institution-branded report templates
- Exportable data for clinical handoffs

V2 is tracked in the [Roadmap](/planning/roadmap) under Feature Ideas.
