## In Progress

### Weekly Reflection — Production Wiring

**Status**: Phase 1 live — wiring phase in progress
**Applies to**: `mind-measure-core`, `mind-measure-mobile-final`

Remaining work before the Weekly Reflection is fully production-ready:

- **Welfare-modified reflection** — alternate email for negative-only weeks (all check-in scores < 40). Warmer tone, university resources surfaced, recap of any mid-week nudges sent. Student-facing only.
- **Opt-out toggle in mobile app** — settings page toggle calling `PATCH /api/profile/settings`. Sets `weekly_reflection_opted_out` on `profiles`.
- **Tier 0 nudge** — gentle email/push when student hasn't checked in for 7+ days. Requires push notification infrastructure (see below).
- **Superuser audit panel** — Wellbeing Sends read-only view in `admin.mindmeasure.co.uk`. Filters by week, university, send type. Mind Measure staff only.
- **Jodie prompt improvements** — named events, sleep probe, positive anchor, social probe, week framing. See [Roadmap](/planning/roadmap).
- **Pro parity** — replicate pipeline in `mind-measure-pro-core` once university is validated.
